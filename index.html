Not all of our interns are used to cold weather, and thats ok! If youre working or traveling in colder climates this winter, taking a few extra precautions can go a long way. Here are some tips to help you keep warm this season: 
Dress in layers
Layers help trap heat and make it easier to adjust as temperatures change.
Listen to your body
Numbness, excessive shivering, and/or fatigue can be early signs of cold stress.
Check your vehicle
Check the tire pressure, fluids, and battery, as cold weather can affect the safety of your vehicle.
